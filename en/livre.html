THE BOOK!

Stefan Alzaris

"Illusionism and Magic" is a book written by magician Stefan Alzaris and published in 2001 by Flammarion. It is an essay that explores the different facets of show magic, as a refined theatrical art that dates back several centuries and combines dreams and illusion.

The book is divided into eight chapters, each dealing with a different aspect of magic: history, psychology, technique, aesthetics, ethics, pedagogy, sociology and philosophy. The author sets out his personal vision of magic, based on a conceptual and creative approach. He also reveals some secrets and tricks for performing magic tricks.

The book is aimed at all lovers of magic, whether beginners or experienced, as well as anyone with an interest in this fascinating art. It offers an in-depth look at the nature and meaning of magic, as well as a practical introduction to this wonderful world.
